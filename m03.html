## More Problems on Software Development? Note: Did students find problems which's not in the list. For example: * User didn't know what they want * User use the wrong IT terms * It works on dev env, but not in the production env. * Broken app on difference env & config.
## Software Development Principles * `DRY` - **D**on't **R**epeat **Y**ourself * `YAGNI` - **Y**ou **A**in't **G**onna **N**eed **I**t Note:
## Communications * Typically the root causes of all evils! * How to make it precise? * How to make it unambiguous? * How to make it clear? * How to communicate changes? * Bosses ***hate*** surprises! Note:
## Requiements Gathering * Customers might not be able to tell what they want clearly. * Customers ***know*** what they want! * What customers' want might change several times! * Make sure to understand their problems instead of obey to their words * Make it clear about ***DO*** and ***DON'T*** * Many project flaws have their root stem from immature requirements. Note:
## Retrospectives * Feedback your thoughts to your team * Mention about * GOOD * BAD * TRY * Discuss about how to improve the team Note:
