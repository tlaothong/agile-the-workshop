### Why Software Fails * Inaccurate understanding of end-user needs * Inability to deal with changing requirements * Modules that don’t fit together * Software that’s hard to maintain or extend * Late discovery of serious project flaws * Poor software quality * Unacceptable software performance * Team members in each other’s way, unable to reconstruct who changed what, when, where, why * An untrustworthy build-and-release process
